| Aspect                | IB Curriculum                                                                                               | A Level Curriculum                                                                                              |
|-----------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Educational System    | International Baccalaureate                                                                                 | Advanced Level                                                                                                  |
